# Code of Conduct

AdocForge contributors must maintain a respectful, constructive, and harassment-free environment.

## Expected Behavior

- Discuss technical decisions with evidence and respect.
- Welcome questions and differing experience levels.
- Give actionable feedback about the work, not the person.
- Respect privacy and avoid publishing another person's private information.
- Accept responsibility, apologize, and correct harm when mistakes occur.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized attention, deliberate intimidation, sustained disruption, and disclosure of private information are not tolerated.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner through their GitHub profile. Reports will be reviewed confidentially and handled according to their context and severity. Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation.

This policy applies in repository spaces and when representing the project elsewhere.
